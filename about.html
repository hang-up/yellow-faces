About Us

What is it?

Yellow Faces is a web page dedicated to linking people with places based on the way they are feeling. Users will find 5 emotions represented by 5 different emojis.

  • Happy? Great! We'll match you with places that will make you even happier.
  • Love? Whether its love from your mom or your girlfriend, we'll help you find places where you can spend a good time with that special someone.
  • Bored? Stop watching Netfilx, go on our website and we'll match you with places that will make you live life to the fullest.
  • Stressed? Fear not! We'll help you find places that will take your mind off school or work.
  • Sad? Don't worry! We'll match you with places that will turn that frown upside down.

How this project came about?

Yellow Faces came alive at ConUHacks 2017. Our team wanted to attract the younger generation to Yellow Pages so we came up with the idea of creating a web page where people could find interesting activites based on the way they are feeling throught emojis.
